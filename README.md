# 🧵 Threadify – On-Demand Tailoring & Alteration Platform

Threadify is a mobile-first tailoring service application designed to connect customers with skilled, verified tailors for custom stitching, alterations, and repairs. The platform simplifies traditional tailoring by offering doorstep pickup & delivery, real-time order tracking, in-app chat, and secure payments.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 👤 For Customers

* Sign up & login (Email)
* Browse tailoring & alteration services
* Schedule doorstep pickup & delivery
* Real-time order tracking
* In-app chat with tailors
* Order history & notifications

### 🧑‍🎨 For Tailors

* Tailor onboarding & profile verification
* Accept / decline orders
* Order management dashboard
* Customer messaging
* Upload portfolio & work samples
* Earnings tracking & payout history
* Ratings & reviews

### 🛠 Platform Features

* Dual-role authentication (Customer & Tailor)
* Role-based dashboards
* Push notifications & alerts
* Scalable backend architecture
* Privacy & data security compliance

---

## 🧭 How It Works

1. Customer selects a service and schedules pickup
2. Tailor accepts the order and starts work
3. Customer tracks progress in real-time
4. Finished garment is delivered to the customer
